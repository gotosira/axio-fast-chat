Placeholder
Placeholder is to guide users to correctly fill the field. This might be considered for omission in some cases. UX/UI designers can omit the text message in placeholder when the main idea of the message is similar to headline or when it doesn’t have additional details.
#Placeholder type 1: to ask question 
##Contexts 
* When to write a message in a more conversational and friendly way.
* When to write guiding messages in general topics, such as food, flight, informal search.
##Samples
###th
####Sample 1
* Text input: คุณต้องการหมายเลขอะไร
####Sample 2
* Text input: อีเมลของคุณคืออะไร
####Sample 3
* Text input: คุณกำลังมองหาเที่ยวบินไหนอยู่
###en
####Sample 1
* Text input: What number are you looking for?
####Sample 2
* Text input: What’s your email?
####Sample 3
* Text input: What flight are you looking for?
